Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions about your appointment? Here are the answers our Franklin Lakes clients ask most.

Book an Appointment
How long does a full set take?

A full set of lash extensions takes between 60–120 minutes (1–2 hours). Refills take 30 minutes to 1 hour. Please arrive 15 minutes before any new appointment.

How should I prepare for my appointment?

It is preferred that you remove all eye makeup prior to arriving. Please come 15 minutes early for new appointments so we have time to design your custom set.

What is the aftercare?

Avoid moisture, extreme heat, sweat, and saunas for the first 24–48 hours after your appointment to keep your lashes looking their best.

How does membership work?

The monthly cost of each membership varies by location. You can freeze your membership for up to 2 months annually, or cancel after your second billing period.

What is the cancellation policy?

Within 12 hours of your appointment time, you’ll be charged 50% of the service price. No-shows will be charged 100%. Please give us at least 12 hours’ notice.

Is there an age requirement?

If you are under 18, you must have a parent or guardian with you at the time of your appointment.
